Welcome.
Please type your commands.
****************************************************

C w h
	Will create a new canvas of width w and height h.

L x1 y1 x2 y2
	Will create a new line from (x1,y1) to (x2,y2).

R x1 y1 x2 y2
	Will create a new rectangle, 	whose upper left corner is (x1,y1) and lower right corner is (x2,y2)

B x y c.
	Will fill the entire area connected to (x,y) with 'colour' c

D
	Demo

X
	Diagonal Lines Demo

Q
	Exit
